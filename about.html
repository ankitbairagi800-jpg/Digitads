Our Story

Built in Indore. Scaled Across Madhya Pradesh.

Digitalads was founded with one simple belief — that small and mid-sized businesses in Indore deserve the same level of digital marketing expertise as large corporates, without paying agency-level retainers for mediocre results.

Founder Ankit Bairagi started as a digital marketing freelancer in Indore, helping local clinics and coaching centers get their first leads through Facebook Ads. As results started rolling in, it became clear that most local businesses were throwing money at digital marketing without a proper strategy.

That's when Digitalads was born — an AI-first, results-obsessed agency that combines cutting-edge tools like n8n, Make.com, ElevenLabs, and CapCut AI with deep local market knowledge to build campaigns that genuinely grow businesses.

Today, we serve 500+ clients across clinics, coaching centers, fitness businesses, and local retail in Indore and across MP — operating as a lean, AI-powered team that punches far above its weight.
