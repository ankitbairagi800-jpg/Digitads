Digital Ads 101

What Are Digital Ads & Why Does Your Business Need Them?

Digital ads are paid advertisements displayed on digital platforms — Google, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube — where your customers spend their time online. Unlike traditional ads (newspapers, hoardings), digital ads allow you to target specific audiences with precision, track every click and lead, and adjust campaigns in real-time.

For businesses in Indore and across India, digital ads are the fastest way to generate leads, calls, and sales — within days, not months.

  • Reach the exact audience most likely to buy from you
  • Pay only when someone clicks on your ad (PPC)
  • Track every lead, call, and sale from your digital ads
  • Start with any budget — scale as you see results
  • Target by location, age, interest, search intent

FAQs

Common Questions About Digital Ads

What are digital ads?
Digital ads are paid advertisements displayed on online platforms like Google, Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube. They let businesses reach targeted audiences based on search intent, demographics, interests and behavior — with complete tracking of every click, lead, and sale.
What is the minimum budget for digital ads?
Minimum effective budget for digital ads: Google Ads ₹15,000–₹30,000/month, Meta Ads ₹8,000–₹15,000/month. For best results combining both platforms, a budget of ₹25,000–₹40,000/month is recommended. Digitalads provides free consultation to determine the right budget for your business goals.
How quickly do digital ads generate results?
Digital ads can generate results quickly. Google Ads typically show initial results within 7–14 days. Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram) usually need 2–4 weeks to optimize. Most clients start seeing consistent leads within 30 days of launching their first digital ad campaign with Digitalads.
What is the difference between digital ads and traditional ads?
Traditional ads (newspaper, TV, hoardings) are expensive, hard to target, and impossible to track accurately. Digital ads offer precise audience targeting, real-time performance tracking, flexible budgets, and measurable ROI — making them far more effective and cost-efficient for businesses of all sizes.
Does Digitalads run digital ads outside of Indore?
Yes! While Digitalads is based in Indore, we run digital ad campaigns for businesses across India and internationally. Our team manages Google Ads, Meta Ads, and YouTube Ads for clients in Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, and across Madhya Pradesh.
